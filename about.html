Our Story

The journey of connecting diaspora talent with Ghana's development needs

2018

Founding Vision

DDIG was founded by a group of Ghanaian diaspora professionals who recognized the untapped potential of diaspora expertise for national development. The vision was simple: create a structured platform to connect diaspora talent with development opportunities in Ghana.

2019

First Projects & Registration

Successfully completed our first three pilot projects in education and healthcare. Officially registered as a non-governmental organization in Ghana, establishing our legal framework and governance structure.

2021

Digital Platform Launch

Launched our digital talent mobilization platform, enabling diaspora professionals worldwide to register their expertise and connect with development projects in Ghana. Expanded to cover multiple sectors including technology, energy, and governance.

2023

International Partnerships

Established formal partnerships with 15+ international diaspora organizations and development agencies. Our network grew to include professionals from 60+ countries across six continents.

2025

Present & Future

Currently supporting 45+ active development projects across Ghana. Our focus is expanding to all 16 regions of Ghana, with emphasis on sustainable development goals and measurable impact through diaspora expertise mobilization.
